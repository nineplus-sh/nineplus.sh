ninePLUS is a way to revitalize why I enjoy software. For the past years, software design and functionality has been very plain. But recently, I've seen software I use slowly drift back to designs I like. I'm not saying we should bring back skeuomorphism. But we should bring back fun.

I don't think that's realistic for most developers, unfortunately. So I'm going to build in public, right here, and see what happens. A lot of what you're going to see will be unfinished, I tend to get distracted and procrastinate. But I hope you get what I'm going for, and I hope you like it.
